Frequently Asked Questions

How does SunTek Reaction PPF “self-heal”?

 The film’s top layer uses advanced polymers that cause light scratches and swirl marks to disappear with heat—either from the sun or warm water.

How long does the film last?

SunTek Reaction PPF is backed by a 12-year warranty, but many owners see effective protection for even longer with proper care.

How does SunTekCan I wash my car as usual?

Yes! Hand washing or touchless washes are best. Avoid automatic washes with brushes, and wait 7 days after installation before your first wash.

Does PPF prevent rock chips?

Absolutely. PPF is specifically engineered to absorb and disperse impacts from rocks, gravel, and road debris, dramatically reducing chips and scratches.

Will it turn yellow or peel over time?

No—SunTek Reaction is warrantied for 12 years against yellowing, cracking, or peeling. Our climate-controlled installation and ceramic boost top coat help it look better, longer.

Can I combine PPF with ceramic coating?

Yes! In fact, we include a complimentary ceramic boost with every install for added shine and easy cleaning.

Is the film removable?

Yes. If needed, PPF can be safely removed by our team without damaging your paint.

Why Choose Paint Protection Film?

Colorado’s warm summers and cold, snowy winters can be quite taxing on your vehicle. Not only are we closer to the sun with all it’s harmful UV, but our Colorado vehicles must endure constant rock chips on dry roads, followed by corrosive winter road chemicals when it snows.
